Help and Support

How do I sign up for an account?
To sign up for an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the page. Then, fill out the registration form with your details and click "Sign Up".
How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
How do I enroll in a course?
To enroll in a course, browse through the available courses and click on the one you're interested in. Then, click on the "Enroll" button and follow the prompts to complete the enrollment process.
